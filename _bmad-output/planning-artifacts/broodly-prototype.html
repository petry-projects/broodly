Safety First

Newbie

Before you open the hive

Confirm your safety gear and conditions.

✓ Wearing full bee suit with veil secured
✓ Gloves on, no exposed skin
✓ Smoker lit and producing cool white smoke
Hive tool and frame grip ready
🧤 Tip: Light your smoker 5 minutes before opening. Use pine needles or burlap for fuel.
