Important Notice

⚠
Advisory-Only Guidance
Broodly provides decision-support recommendations based on your observations, regional context, and best practices. These recommendations are advisory only and should not replace:
• Professional veterinary advice
• Local beekeeping association guidance
• Your own judgment and experience
• Compliance with local regulations
⚠ Recommendations include confidence levels. Lower confidence means more uncertainty — always consider the fallback guidance provided.
Treatment recommendations are filtered by your region's regulatory status where data is available. In regions without regulatory data, a notice will be shown.
